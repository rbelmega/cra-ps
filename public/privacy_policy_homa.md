# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: December 22, 2025_

This Privacy Policy describes how **Homa** (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

We value your privacy and are committed to keeping your data safe, minimal, and fully under your control.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

Homa is designed to work **without collecting personal data**.

All information created in the app is stored **locally on your device** and may include:

- Tasks and reminders
- Zones (rooms or areas)
- Equipment information
- Task history and optional notes
- App settings and preferences

We **do not collect, store, or process**:
- Names or usernames
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos or media files
- Payment or billing information

---

## 2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used **only to provide core app functionality**, including:

- Creating and managing tasks
- Scheduling reminders
- Displaying task history
- Improving usability and reliability

All data remains **on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on your device.
- We do not use external servers to store your information.
- We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Homa does **not** use:
- Advertising SDKs
- Analytics or tracking tools
- Third-party data brokers

If any third-party services are introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

---

## 5. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data.

You can delete your data at any time by:
- Removing tasks or zones within the app
- Uninstalling the app, which permanently removes all locally stored data

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Homa does not knowingly collect or store personal information from children under the age of 13.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
